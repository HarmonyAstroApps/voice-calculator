Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

At HarmonyAstroApps, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Voice Calculator app collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Speech Recognition Data

Voice Calculator uses Apple's Speech Recognition technology to convert your spoken words into text for calculations. When you use the voice feature, audio data is sent to Apple's servers for processing. This data is used solely to provide the speech-to-text functionality and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not store your voice recordings or transcripts on our own servers.

2. Data Collection & Usage

We do not collect personal usage data that is linked to your identity. We may collect anonymous analytics data (such as app crashes, feature usage statistics, and device locale) to help us improve the app's performance and stability. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

3. Local Storage

Your calculation history and settings (such as theme preferences and decimal places) are stored locally on your device. You can clear your history at any time within the app settings.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for analytics and performance monitoring. These services may collect information about your device and app usage patterns.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@harmonyastroapps.com.
