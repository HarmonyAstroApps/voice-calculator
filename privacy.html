Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

At HarmonyAstroApps, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Voice Calculator app collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Speech Recognition Data

Voice Calculator uses Apple's Speech Recognition technology to convert your spoken words into text for calculations. When you use the voice feature, audio data is processed by Apple's on-device or cloud-based speech engine. This data is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not store your voice recordings or transcripts on our own servers.

2. Data Collection & Usage

We do not collect personal usage data that is linked to your identity. We may collect anonymous analytics data (such as app crashes, feature usage statistics, and device locale) to help improve the app's performance and stability. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

The following third-party services may be used for analytics and app improvement:

  • Firebase Analytics -- anonymous usage statistics
  • Firebase Crashlytics -- crash reports for stability improvements
  • RevenueCat -- subscription management (processes purchase data through Apple)

3. Local Storage

Your calculation history, settings (such as theme preferences, decimal places, and tax rate), and app preferences are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device. You can clear your history at any time within the app settings.

4. Microphone & Permissions

Voice Calculator requests the following permissions:

  • Microphone -- required for voice-based calculations
  • Speech Recognition -- required to convert speech to text
  • Siri -- optional, for launching the app via voice commands

These permissions are used solely to provide the app's core voice functionality. You can revoke them at any time in your device's Settings.

5. Subscriptions & Purchases

If you purchase a subscription or make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. RevenueCat is used to manage subscription status and does not collect personally identifiable information.

6. Children's Privacy

Voice Calculator does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@harmonyastroapps.com.
