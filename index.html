Your Privacy Matters

🔊 Voice Data

Voice audio is processed by Apple's Speech Recognition on-device or via Apple's servers. We never store your voice recordings or transcripts.

📱 Local Storage

Your calculation history, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device. You can clear them anytime.

🔒 No Personal Data

We do not collect personal data linked to your identity. Only anonymous crash and usage analytics help us improve the app.
