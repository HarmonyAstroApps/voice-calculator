About HarmonyAstroApps

We're an independent iOS developer making focused utility apps that respect your time, your privacy, and your attention.

HarmonyAstroApps builds small, useful iOS apps for iPhone and iPad. We're not a startup, not VC-backed, and not trying to become a platform. We just like making tools we'd want to use ourselves, shipping them, and moving on.

Our flagship app is Voice Calculator — a hands-free calculator that lets you speak your math instead of typing it. It's designed for cooking, shopping, quick estimates, accessibility, and any situation where your hands are busy but your voice is free.

What we believe

Focused, not bloated

Each app does one thing well. No dashboards, no upsells, no features you didn't ask for.

Privacy by default

Your data stays on your device. We don't track you, profile you, or sell ads against you.

Calm by design

No dark patterns, no nag screens, no manufactured urgency. The app waits for you.

Honest pricing

Apps are reasonably priced and transparent. No subscription traps for single-purpose tools.

How we build

Voice Calculator, like all our apps, is built natively in Swift for iOS using Apple's SwiftUI framework. Speech recognition is powered by Apple's on-device Speech framework, which means audio stays on your device whenever possible. We use standard Apple APIs, not third-party trackers or SDKs that phone home.

We test every release on real devices, across iPhone and iPad, in light and dark mode, with VoiceOver enabled, and with Dynamic Type turned up. We care about accessibility because voice-first apps are, by nature, an accessibility tool — and we want them to work for everyone.

Who we are

HarmonyAstroApps is a small independent developer based in India, shipping apps to a global audience on the App Store. We built Voice Calculator because no existing calculator handled continuous voice input well enough for real-world use — so we made one.
