Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a voice calculation?
Tap the microphone button and start speaking your numbers and operations naturally. The app listens continuously -- just say numbers followed by operations like "5 plus 27 plus 35000".
What does the "Read" button do?
Tap "Read" to hear your full calculation list read back to you. This lets you verify every entry without looking at the screen -- perfect for catching mistakes.
How do I add tax to my calculation?
Just say "plus tax" or "tax" during a calculation. You can set your tax rate in Settings. The app will automatically apply the percentage to your current total.
How do I export my calculations?
Tap the share icon at the top of the calculator. You can export as CSV (for Excel/Google Sheets) or plain text (for notes and messages).
How do I copy the result quickly?
Long-press the result number at the bottom of the screen. It will be copied to your clipboard instantly.
Can I see my previous calculations?
Yes! Tap the history icon (clock) at the top right. You'll see a full audit trail of all past calculations with timestamps.
What's the difference between Basic and Scientific mode?
Basic mode follows standard left-to-right calculation (1 + 2 × 3 = 9). Scientific mode respects mathematical order of operations (1 + 2 × 3 = 7).

Troubleshooting

Voice recognition isn't working
  1. Check that microphone permission is enabled: Settings → Voice Calculator → Microphone
  2. Check that speech recognition is enabled: Settings → Voice Calculator → Speech Recognition
  3. Ensure you're not in a very noisy environment
  4. Try restarting the app
  5. Make sure your device is connected to the internet (required for some speech recognition features)
The app isn't recognizing my numbers correctly
  1. Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  2. Check that the correct language is selected in your device's Speech settings
  3. For large numbers, try speaking them in segments (e.g. "thirty five thousand" instead of individual digits)
  4. Reduce background noise if possible
App crashes or freezes
  1. Make sure you're running the latest version of Voice Calculator
  2. Update to the latest iOS version
  3. Force-close the app and reopen it
  4. Restart your device
  5. If the issue persists, contact us with details about what happened
